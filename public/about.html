Six Interesting Findings

  1. Capital River has the most crimes and is one of the smallest neighborhoods in Saint Paul
  2. When you search for Como neighborhood, there is one in Minneapolis and one in Saint Paul
  3. The most popular crimes in the St.Thomas neighborhood of Union Park are property crimes.
  4. Even though Capitol River has the most crimes it only has around the same amount of violent crimes as any other neighborhood
  5. The northern part of St.Paul has the most violent crime with Frogtown/North End/Payne/Phalen averaging around 25-35 violent crime where other neighborhoods are around 10-15
  6. Summit Hill has the least amount of crime with 72, but St.Anthony has the second least crime with 103 but is three times as large as Summit Hill
